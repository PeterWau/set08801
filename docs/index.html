Register

We only require you to enter your name and you are ready to go.

Dashboard

Our Dashboard will guide your throug your learning journey.

Add training videos

  1. Go to Training Videos
  2. Click Add Video
  3. Enter:
    • URL (YouTube link)
    • Coach (e.g., Danny Maude)
    • Skill Focus (a short tag like start-downswing)

Record practice seesions

  1. Go to Record Practice Session
  2. Select a Club (Driver, 3, 5, 7, 9, PW)
  3. Enter one Skill Focus (a single thought for the swing)
  4. Add shots:
    • Distance (number)
    • Shape (Hook, Draw, Straight, Slice)
    • Contact (Clean, Top, Fat)
  5. Click Save Session

Check your club distances

  1. Go to Distance Report
  2. It shows a breakdown of average club distance that you can take to the course.
